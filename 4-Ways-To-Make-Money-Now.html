Business 4 Ways To Make Money Now
By JimAdams Jan 04, 2019
32 42 7

DISCLAIMER: This does not guarantee you can make money. It involves a lot of risks and highly likely that you will lose money. Success depends on personal luck, skills and talent. Good luck!


1. Make YouTube Videos
YouTube is the 2nd largest search engine in the world – that’s a lot of eyeballs available to watch your content!

Brainstorm some ideas on what you could create videos on, set up a YouTube account, and start creating content.

You’ll find the most surprising things on YouTube, so chances are, if you think you can do something interesting, others will as well. You never quite know until you put it out there for the world to see.

In general, tutorials and lifestyle videos tend to perform well.

How much can you make: $10+ in ad revenue (maybe more in sponsorships), depending on how many views you have

Time needed: A few hours to produce and edit each video



2. Start a Podcast
According to Pat Flynn, one of the most popular podcasters, there are several steps to having a successful podcast:

Choose a topic that you enjoy and can commit to
Define your show’s description and artwork
Test (and test again) your equipment
Create a plan and journey for your episodes
Record your episodes
Edit and publish your episodes
Launch your podcast to your audience and heavily promote
You can even find sponsors to pay you for product placements. Also, placing ads on your podcast episode pages can generate passive income.

How much can you make: $25 to $50 per 1,000 listeners in advertising revenue

Time needed: A few hours to produce each episode




3. Sign up for MyPoints, Swagbucks, and/or Rakuten
We mentioned these cash back and survey sites earlier, but they’re worth repeating because you can get free money instantly.

MyPoints ($10 for signing up)
Swagbucks ($5 for signing up)
Rakuten ($10 for signing up)
These are bonuses you’ll get just for creating an account. That’s it!

How much can you make: $25 for signing up for all three

Time needed: 15 minutes to set up accounts for all three



4. Refer 5 Friends to Ibotta and Make $25
For every 5 friends that you can get to sign up for your team, you’ll get $25. This is perfect for your whole family to partake in, or you can even start a team for your friends.

You can also receive a teamwork bonus if you save enough.

To get started, you can join our savvy savers team!

How much can you make: $10 for signing up and $25 for every 5 people you refer

Time needed: 10 minutes of phone calls and a few minutes to set up your account
